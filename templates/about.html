Malaviya Sports Tournament '18
About Us
One of the biggest sports tournaments in Rajasthan, Malaviya Sports Tournament (MST) started in 2007. In its inaugural edition, around 300 participants from 12 institutions took part in it. Over the course of years, MST has grown a lot, with the number of participants as well as institutes increasing exponentially. In 2017, MST was named MSF (Malaviya Sports Fest) in which more than 1100 participants from 31 institutes made their presence felt. This year, the event has been rechristened as MST (Malaviya Sports Tournament). MST'18 is scheduled from 23-25 Feb 2018. The various sports in which participants will fight for the prestige of their respective institutions are — Cricket, Football, Basketball, Volleyball, Lawn Tennis, Table Tennis, Badminton, Chess, Kabaddi,Body Building, Weight Lifting and Power Lifting Apart from the competitive events, there will be enough to keep the audience engaged. There will be a Fashion show, fun games & events and yes, of course, the most awaited and exciting celebrity nights. MST'18 promises to enthral its audience and will definitely live up to its hype and expectations.
