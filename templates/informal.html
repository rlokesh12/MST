Day 1
KANCHA
Nostalgia of the good old childhood days collecting and playing with shiny marbles. Well, we take you back to those memories and give you a chance to relive your childhood by bringing to you the game of marbles.
CRAB SOCCER
Sounds weird? Yes, but a totally fun filled event where you have to play soccer with your bodies on the ground and backs facing the ground. Now it might sound even more interesting as it will require teamwork and effort. Try and beat you opponent by getting the ball across their goal.
Day 2
Mind the face
Both the players will throw water filled sponges at each other's face one after the other. Check out you hitting and dodging skills while having some great fun simultaneously.
Soapy football
Slippery soap mat + football on that = escalated amount of fun. The player amongst the two who goals first is the champ !
Mega snake and ladder
Fun game where participants will play snake and ladders on a large mat. Who reaches the top first is the winner.
Escape the room
Do you love mazes ? Then ribbon in the room is just for you ! One player enters the room full of ribbons. All tou need to do is save yourself without touching them and cross the room. Hmmm seems like a maze !
Day 3
Stamp the balloon
Bursting balloons is fun, but it's even more interesting when you have to protect them as well. 4 balloons on each legs is tied using threads and the players are confined to a circle of diameter 10 feet. Each player have to save their own balloon and try to burst their opponents balloons using only their legs.
